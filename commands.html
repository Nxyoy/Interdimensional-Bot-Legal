Commands

🎮 Fun
  • /8ball – Ask the magic 8-ball a question!
  • /rate – Get a super accurate rating of something
💼 Economy
  • /work – Do some work to earn money.
  • /crime – Attempt a criminal act for cash.
  • /beg – Beg for money like a cosmic peasant.
  • /rob – Try to rob another user!
  • /balance – Check your balance (cash, bank, total).
  • /deposit – Deposit cash from your hand into your bank.
  • /withdraw – Withdraw cash from your bank to your hand.
🎲 Games
  • /tictactoe – Challenge another user to a game of Tic-Tac-Toe!
  • /roulette – Play a game of roulette!
  • /blackjack – Play a game of blackjack against the bot!
  • /catsino – Wager your cats for a chance at a higher rarity!
  • /catsino-log – View your cat gambling history.
🛠️ Tools
  • /invite – Get the bot's invite link
  • /help – Display help menu
  • /debugsync – Debug command to re-sync all global slash commands and list them.
  • /serverstats – Displays CPU, RAM, and latency usage.
  • /changelog – View the latest changes.
🪯 Moderation
  • /kick – Kick a user
  • /ban – Ban a user
  • /mute – Mute a user
  • /unmute – Unmute a user
🔊 Music
  • /play – Play a song or add it to the queue.
  • /skip – Skip the current song.
  • /loop – Manage music looping.
  • /playlist – Manage your saved playlists.
  • /stop – Stop playback and clear the queue.
  • /pause – Pause the current song.
  • /resume – Resume the paused song.
  • /queue – Show the current song queue.
👑 Admin
  • /forcecat – [ADMIN ONLY] Forces a cat to spawn.
  • /clearcat – [ADMIN ONLY] Clear any active cat in this guild (Admin only)
  • /setcatchannel – [ADMIN ONLY] Enable or disable cat spawning in a specific channel.
  • /changespawnrate – [ADMIN ONLY] Change the cat spawn interval for this server.
  • /setadminrole – [ADMIN ONLY] Set a custom role that can run admin commands.
  • /set-daily-income-roles – [ADMIN ONLY] Set roles that can collect daily income.
✨ General
  • /greet – Greets the user
  • /brew – Brew a coffee
