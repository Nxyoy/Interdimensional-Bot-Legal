Commands

🎮 Fun
  • /8ball – Ask the magic ball a question
  • /rate – Get a random rating of anything
💼 Economy
  • /work – Earn some cash
  • /crime – Take a risk for money
  • /beg – Beg for spare change
  • /balance – View your wallet & bank
  • /deposit – Move money into the bank
  • /withdraw – Take money from the bank
🛠️ Tools
  • /invite – Get the bot invite link
  • /help – Display help menu
🪯 Moderation
  • /kick – Kick a user
  • /ban – Ban a user
  • /mute – Mute a user
  • /unmute – Unmute a user
🔊 Music
  • /play – Play from YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud
  • /skip – Skip the current song
  • /queue – Show the queue
