Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This policy explains how Interdimensional Bot (#6122) uses data.

1. What We Collect

We store:

  • User IDs (for economy tracking, command responses)
  • Guild IDs (for music queues, playlists)
  • Economy balances (hand, bank)
  • Custom ratings and data tied to your user ID

2. Why We Collect It

Data is collected to provide core features like:

  • Personalized economy commands
  • Saved music playback queues/playlists
  • Custom command interactions (e.g., /rate)

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or monetize your data. It is only used internally by the bot.

4. Data Retention

Data is stored as long as the bot is active or until a deletion request is made by the user or Discord server owner.

5. Contact

If you have questions or want your data removed, contact the bot owner via Discord.

By using Interdimensional Bot, you agree to this privacy policy.
