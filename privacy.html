Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Interdimensional Bot (Discord tag: #6122), developed and maintained by independent developer Nxyoy, collects, uses, and stores information in the course of providing services to users on Discord.

1. Information We Collect

  • User IDs: Used to manage features such as the economy system, user-specific music playlists, and command statistics.
  • Guild (Server) IDs: Used to store guild-specific data such as music queues, warnings, and configuration settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used exclusively for bot operations, including:

  • Saving and retrieving economy balances
  • Managing custom playlists and music queues
  • Handling guild moderation tools like warnings
  • Enhancing personalized bot features and experiences

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on our systems and is never shared, sold, rented, or distributed to third parties. We take appropriate technical measures to protect this data against unauthorized access or loss.

4. No Sensitive Data

We do not collect any sensitive personal information such as names, email addresses, passwords, payment information, or private messages.

5. Consent

By using Interdimensional Bot, you agree to this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined above.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future to reflect new features or compliance requirements. Changes will be communicated through updates to this page.
